Components Required:
	ESP32 board
	DHT11 sensor (3 pins: VCC, GND, and DATA)
	Jumper wires

DHT11 Sensor:
	Connect the VCC pin of the DHT11 sensor to the 3.3V pin on the ESP32.
	Connect the GND pin of the DHT11 sensor to the GND pin on the ESP32.
	Connect the DATA pin of the DHT11 sensor to GPIO 15 on the ESP32 (or any other GPIO pin).

ESP32:
	Ensure that your ESP32 is powered via USB or external power, as per your project setup.
