Components Required:
	ESP32 board
	PIR Motion Sensor
	1 LED
	Jumper wires

PIR Sensor:
	Connect VCC pin of PIR sensor to 3.3V on ESP32
	Connect GND pin of PIR Sensor to GND on ESP32
	Connect OUT pin of PIR  to GPIO 34 on ESP32

	Anode (long leg) to GPI O5 on ESP32
