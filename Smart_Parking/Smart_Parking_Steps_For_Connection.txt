Components Required:
	ESP32 board (with built-in LED)
	2 IR Sensors
	Jumper wires

IR Sensor:
	Connect the VCC pin of the IR sensor to the 3.3V pins on the ESP32.
	Connect the GND pin of the IR sensor to the GND pins on the ESP32.
	Connect the OUT pin (analog output) of the IR sensor-1 to GPIO 34 on the ESP32.
	Connect the OUT pin (analog output) of the IR sensor-2 to GPIO 35 on the ESP32.
